Elixir Pharmaceutical Lines

Musculoskeletal

We produce a diverse range of products for treating and managing a range of muscle and skeletal disorders. Different treatments across this line are carefully and effectively crafted to treat the pain and inflammation of muscles, cartilage, and joints.

Anti-infective

Anti-infective medical treatments have revolutionized healthcare and are now critical to curing and preventing many life-threatening viral, bacterial, and fungal infections. We produce the latest generations of anti-infectives used to treat and fend off a wide variety of infections, such as nosocomial pneumonia, community-acquired pneumonia, skin and soft tissue infections, and diabetic foot infections.

Neuroscience

Elixir’s neuro-science line is focused on serving patients living with psychiatric disorders and neurological conditions. This line’s development was guided by an early bird mindset to create partnerships between patients, healthcare professionals, and healthcare systems. Using a wide range of molecules that cover all disorders, from common to rare, the line aims to deliver life-enhancing solutions that boost people’s mental health across Elixir’s footprint.
