Our Story
Elixir Pharma started with the dream of Hany Aly, whose love for trade was enrooted in him since childhood.

The dream first came to life with a project he founded in MadagascarcalledTRUST PHARMA, which helped him create trusted connections and gain a deeper understanding of the country and of other African nations’ needs.

Additional connections with Egyptian companies allowed Hany to import medication into Madagascar and contribute to the improvement of the country’s health.

A temporary return to the multinational world where he once worked and a quick climb up the ladder helped Hany expand his network, and he was able to return to his original mission of realizing his life-long dream, a passion that has turned today into Elixir Pharma.
Who are we
The word ‘Elixir’ has Latin, Greek, and Arabic origins and is defined as “a potion with magical powers.”

The same description applies to modern-day medicine, as its capabilities continue to grow and introduce life-changing improvements to patients’ lives.

Elixir Pharma is an emerging shareholding pharmaceutical company that specializes in the provision of varied commercial and scarce medication. Founded in 2018, we have rapidly grown by innovatively creating a diversified portfolio of products on the back of the ambition and drive of our highly qualified team.

As a consumer-centric organization, we aim to empower our communities and facilitate access to cost-effective medication across the nation, with our next step seeing us expand across Africa. As such, we work towards creating long-term partnerships with our stakeholders to maximize mutual benefits and create a lasting impact that improves people’s quality of life everywhere we go.

Our Values

High-Quality Operations

At Elixir, we will always favor quality over quantity, and our manufacturing, packaging, and distribution processes strictly adhere to advanced industry standards.

Patient-Centricity

Patients are at the center of our operations. Every action we take aims to ensure that patients and members of our communities are at the receiving end of the best and most effective pharmaceutical solutions available.

Respect and Integrity

We meet all stakeholder interactions and requests with transparency, compassion, and unfiltered honesty to build solid bonds and further reinforce ourselves as a trustworthy organization.

Passion for Excellence

Every Elixir team member strives to provide exceptional work and deliver high-quality results at every possible opportunity. We build our successes on the professional excellence of our team members.

Our Purpose

People deserve to live, not just survive.

We believe in every person’s right to medicine, but we also seek to improve people’s lives beyond medical treatment. Through communication, we aim to build a platform that cares for people on both; physical and mental levels. We invest in growing ideas, recommendations, insights, and support to create a positive environment that strengthens the tie between people’s health and happiness. To achieve this, we are also committed to selling medicine that treats ailments conveniently. We do not believe that a person must suffer on their way to recovery, and we make sure that all our products reflect this ideology.
