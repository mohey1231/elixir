Our
Story
The story of Elixir Pharma begins with the dream of a young man with an entrenched love for trade that started in his childhood. This dream has grown with a project in Madagascar (Trust Pharma) which offered its Founder, Hany, trusted connections and a deeper understanding of the country and the African needs. Further connections with Egyptian companies have allowed Hany to import medications into Madagascar and improve the country's health. A temporary return to the multinational world acted as a quick climb up to the ladder that expanded Hany's network, which turned his passion into Elixir Pharma.
Who are
we ?!
"Elixir" has Latin, Greek, and Arabic origins, defined as "a potion with magical powers." Elixir is modern-day's medicine. Elixir Pharma is an emerging shareholding pharmaceutical company specializing in the provision of various commercial and scarce medications. Founded in 2018, we have rapidly grown with the ambition and drive of our highly qualified team. As a consumer-centric organization, we aim to empower our communities and facilitate access to cost-effective medications across the nation and expand across Africa. As such, we fuel the creation of long-term partnerships with our stakeholders to maximize mutual benefits and create lasting value for everyone involved.

Our Vision

We are providing groundbreaking, affordable, and innovative pharmaceutical solutions across Egypt and Africa to improve the quality of people’s lives.

Our Mission

To reach the forefront of the Egyptian pharmaceutical market and lead pharmaceutical exports in Africa.

OUR Values

Customer Centricity

Customers are the center of our operations. Every action we take aims to ensure that customers and our community members are at the receiving end of effective pharmaceutical solutions.

Respect and Integrity

We meet all stakeholder interactions and requests with transparency, compassion, and unfiltered honesty, to build solid bonds and further reinforce ourselves as a trustworthy organization.

Passion for Excellence

Every Elixir team member strives to provide exceptional work and deliver high-quality results at every possible opportunity. We build our successes on the professional excellence of our team members.

Our Purpose

People deserve to live, not just survive.

We believe in every person’s right to medicine, but we also seek to improve people’s lives beyond medical treatment. We aim to build a platform that cares for people on both physical and mental levels through communication. We invest in growing ideas, recommendations, insights, and support to create a positive environment that strengthens the tie between people’s health and happiness. To achieve this, we are also committed to selling medicine that treats ailments conveniently. We do not believe that a person must suffer on their way to recovery, and we make sure that all our products reflect this ideology.
