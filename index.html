CEO Message

It started with the dream of a young man, who enrooted his love of trade since childhood. The dream grow bigger with a project in Madcascar (TRUST PHARMA) which produced trusted connections, a deeper understanding of the country and the African needs. Further connections with Egyptian companies allowed Hany to import medication into Madagascar, and contribute to the improvement of the country’s health Temporary return to the multinational world and another quick climb up the ladder expanded Hany’s network, and Soon this passion has turned this to be Elixir pharma.
